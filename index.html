About Me

I am a recent graduate of Eastern Illinois University, where I graduated with a BS in Computer Science. I have worked as an intern with the major aerospace company Northrop Grumman. Above all, I am an enthusiastic programmer who loves taking opportunities to learn new things. It is my hope that this website will effectively convey why I am the right employee for your company.

Click the tabs below to learn more about my education, skills, and experience.

  • My Education

    I chose Eastern Illinois University as the place where I would begin building my future. I favored EIU's homey feeling and its closely knit community over the larger universities nearby. As a Computer Science student, I learned about the fundamentals of computer architecture, data structures, and modern software design. I enjoyed challenging, high-quality classes with topics such as cryptography, coding theory, and operating systems, and I had the opportunity to meet some outstanding professors. I graduated in August 2017 with a BS in Mathematics and Computer Science.

  • My Skills

    Throughout my education and work history I have accrued many skills valuable for all information technology jobs. I have mastery of a variety of modern programming languages including C, Java, and Python. At Eastern Illinois University I studied computer science and mathematics, giving me a mathematical background superior to that of the average computer science graduate. I have gained essential communication skills from my time as a customer service representative for my university. During my internship with Northrop Grumman, I also gained valuable knowledge of group work in the IT field.     As a university student, I received experience with the fundamentals of all technology work.
